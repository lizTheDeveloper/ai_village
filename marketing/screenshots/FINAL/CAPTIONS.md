# Multiverse: The End of Eternity — Marketing Screenshots

## Summary
10 screenshots showcasing the depth and breadth of MVEE's systems.

---

### 01_multiverse_gateway.png
**The Multiverse Gateway**
Choose your path through infinite possibilities. Create brand-new universes from nothing, or join existing ones to play as a deity guiding civilizations. A clean, dark UI that sets the cosmic tone.

### 02_species_discovery.png
**Species Discovery by Biome**
Twelve unique species discovered across five distinct biomes — Swamp, Forest, Ocean, Desert, and Grassland. Each species evolves independently with unique traits, names, and behaviors. Shows the game's emergent biodiversity.

### 03_admin_console.png
**AI Village Admin Console**
A powerful developer/deity dashboard with 38+ specialized system tabs: Magic & Divinity, Combat, Time & Timelines, Politics & Governance, Dreams & Sleep, Consciousness, Corruption, and more. Shows the sheer scale of simulated systems.

### 04_magic_divinity.png
**Magic & Divinity System**
Query agent magic status, inspect registered spells, track deity power levels, and simulate divine interventions. Gods accumulate faith and can unlock supernatural abilities via spell casting and divinity mechanics.

### 05_time_travel.png
**Time Travel & Universe Branching**
Save and rewind game state to any point in history. Fork universes to explore alternate timelines. Create manual saves, load snapshots, and branch parallel realities — the ultimate "what if" game mechanic.

### 06_soul_gallery.png
**Soul Gallery — Eternal Archive**
An archive of 41 reincarnated souls across multiple lives. Legendary souls like Orion (50+ incarnations) and Sparrow persist through history. First-incarnation markers, dimensional tracking, and soul lineages create deep lore.

### 07_combat_system.png
**Combat System**
Real-time combat simulation with faction battles, fleet combat, and agent skirmishes. Query active battles, simulate outcomes, and track combat power for every faction. Full combat lifecycle management.

### 08_planet_creation.png
**Planet Creation Wizard**
Design new worlds: choose planet type (Terrestrial, Ocean, Desert, Volcanic, Jungle), art style (NES Classic, Game Boy, SNES, Game Boy Advance, PlayStation 1, Nintendo 64), and name your creation before generating a fully realized world.

### 09_roadmap_pipelines.png
**Roadmap & Development Pipelines**
Track game development milestones with work orders, completion status, and pipeline tracking. Foundation, World Generation, First Agent, and more — the game's own development visible from within.

### 10_memories_consciousness.png
**Memories & Consciousness**
Deep dive into agent psychology: view traumatic memories, cherished moments, and complex inner lives. Each agent has a unique consciousness with persistent memories that carry across reincarnations.
