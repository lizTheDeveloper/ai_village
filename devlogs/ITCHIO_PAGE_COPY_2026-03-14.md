## Description (full page copy)

**Multiverse: The End of Eternity** is a browser-based civilization simulator where every villager thinks for themselves.

[**Play the beta now — no download required →**](https://play.multiversestudios.xyz/mvee/)

Inspired by Dwarf Fortress and driven by real AI language models, MVEE lets you watch — or steer — a village of autonomous agents as they grow crops, raise buildings, forge alliances, conduct research, and ultimately determine the fate of their world across time.

This is not a god game. You are not omnipotent. The agents have their own goals, memories, and opinions — and they will disagree with you.

---

### What makes MVEE different

**Every agent is AI-powered.** Villagers reason, plan, and speak using large language models. They remember what happened, form relationships, and make decisions you didn't script.

**Dwarf Fortress depth, browser convenience.** Deep simulation of economics, sociology, technology trees, and resource chains — all running in your browser with no install. Just open and play.

**Emergent history.** No two playthroughs unfold the same way. Alliances form and collapse. Technologies get reinvented. Civilizations rise — and fall — on the strength of their people's choices.

**Magic, divinity, and genetics.** 25+ magic paradigms (from Shinto animism to Allomancy), a fully emergent religion system where gods arise from worship, genetics and inheritance, reincarnation, and family dynasties.

**Time and consequence.** Decisions ripple across generations. What your founders built (or burned) shapes what their descendants inherit. Fork the multiverse to explore "what if" scenarios — or watch parallel timelines diverge.

**Observe or intervene.** Spectate the autonomous village from above, or take direct control of any agent and live inside the simulation.

The end of eternity is coming. How your civilization meets it is up to them — and you.
