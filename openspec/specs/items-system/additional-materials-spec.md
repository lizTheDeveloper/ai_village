# Additional Fantasy & Sci-Fi Materials Specification

**Created**: 2026-01-03
**Status**: Proposal
**Context**: Expanding material variety beyond existing 31 surreal materials + 6 quantum/temporal materials

---

## Current Material Inventory (37 Total)

### By Rarity
- **Common (10)**: Wood, Clay, Stone, Sand, Coal, Paper, Salt, Rust, Water, Flowing Sand
- **Uncommon (9)**: Giant Mushroom, Sugar Brick, Chocolate, Ice, Steel, Glass, Bone, Silk, Wax, Coral
- **Rare (9)**: Flesh Brick, Living Clockwork, Frozen Music, Resonant Crystal, Smoke, Amber, Porcelain, Poison, Solidified Time (new), Crystallized Emotion (new), Fossilized Thought (new), Petrified Light (new), Condensed Probability (new)
- **Legendary (6)**: Eternal Flame, Shadow Essence, Dream Crystal, Memory Crystal, Diamond, Quantum Foam (new)

### Gaps Identified
1. **No mythic tier materials** (highest rarity)
2. **Limited sci-fi materials** (mostly fantasy/surreal)
3. **No anti-magic materials** (for balance)
4. **Limited creature-derived materials** (dragonscale, chitin, etc.)
5. **No celestial/cosmic materials** (starlight, void, meteors)
6. **No transformation materials** (shapeshifting, transmutation)

## Proposed Materials by Category

## Category 1: Legendary Metals (Combat/Crafting)

### 1. Mithril
- **Tier**: 7-8
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Base Value**: 2500
- **Properties**:
  - Extremely light (weight: 0.5, but strong as steel)
  - Never rusts or tarnishes (decayRate: 0)
  - Conducts magic efficiently
  - Structurally strong (90) with incredible durability
- **Harvesting**: Deep underground veins (extremely rare), ancient dwarven ruins
- **Uses**: Elite weapons, armor, magical conductors
- **Aesthetics**: Silvery-white shimmer, cool to touch, faint blue glow in moonlight
- **Why Add**: Classic fantasy metal, enables high-tier equipment progression

### 2. Adamantine
- **Tier**: 9
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Base Value**: 5000
- **Properties**:
  - Hardest material known (structuralStrength: 100+)
  - Nearly indestructible (durability: 1000+)
  - Extremely heavy (weight: 8.0)
  - Resistant to all elements (temperatureResistance: 100)
- **Harvesting**: Volcanic cores, meteor impacts, plane of earth
- **Uses**: Fortress walls, vault doors, legendary weapons
- **Aesthetics**: Jet black with metallic sheen, rings when struck
- **Why Add**: Ultimate defense material, represents peak metalworking

### 3. Orichalcum
- **Tier**: 6-7
- **Rarity**: Rare
- **Base Value**: 800
- **Properties**:
  - Magical metal (conducts mana)
  - Red-gold color
  - Self-repairing (decayRate: -0.01)
  - Moderate strength (75)
- **Harvesting**: Ancient ruins, underwater cities, magical forges
- **Uses**: Magic amplifiers, automaton cores, enchanted gear
- **Aesthetics**: Red-gold metal, warm to touch, pulses with contained energy
- **Why Add**: Atlantean metal, bridges magic and technology

## Category 2: Celestial Materials (Cosmic/Space)

### 4. Starmetal (Meteorite)
- **Tier**: 6-7
- **Rarity**: Epic
- **Base Value**: 1500
- **Properties**:
  - Extraterrestrial origin
  - Contains trace cosmic energy
  - Very dense (weight: 6.0)
  - Resistant to magic (50% reduction)
- **Harvesting**: Meteor impact sites, fallen stars, sky realms
- **Uses**: Anti-magic weapons, cosmic navigation tools
- **Aesthetics**: Dark gray with iridescent shimmer, tiny star-like sparkles inside
- **Why Add**: Cosmic origin, anti-magic properties for balance

### 5. Starlight Essence
- **Tier**: 8
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Base Value**: 3500
- **Properties**:
  - Captured stellar radiation
  - Weightless (weight: 0.05)
  - Emits perpetual soft light
  - Decays slowly in darkness (decayRate: 0.1)
  - Requires exposure to starlight to maintain
- **Harvesting**: Stellar observatories, star wells, celestial realm
- **Uses**: Eternal lanterns, navigation beacons, revelation spells
- **Aesthetics**: Liquid silver-white light, flows like water, illuminates without heat
- **Why Add**: Complements Petrified Light, enables stellar magic

### 6. Voidstone
- **Tier**: 8-9
- **Rarity**: Legendary
- **Base Value**: 4000
- **Properties**:
  - Pure absence (anti-matter conceptually)
  - Nullifies magic in radius
  - Extremely dangerous (hostility: 0.9)
  - Cold beyond measure
  - Weight paradox (feels heavy and weightless simultaneously: weight: 0)
- **Harvesting**: Void rifts, black holes, entropy zones, edge of universe
- **Uses**: Magic dampening fields, void prisons, anti-magic barriers
- **Aesthetics**: Absolute black that absorbs light, creates visual distortion, whispers of nothingness
- **Why Add**: Counter to magic-heavy gameplay, introduces risk/reward
